The Idea Behind Nepali Ragat

It all started when an idea struck in my head in the 2080 dashain. When i witnessed my brothers working relentlessly for the availibility of blood due to Elephant attack in my area. It rers working relentlessly for the availibility of blood due to Elephant attack in my area. It really hit my heart. We assume ourselves to be technologically advanced but during situations like these we are so outdated. I wanted this to change with my effort. And making a database of blood in our area hit my head. And with some brainstorming, this idea transformed into a website model which I proposed to my friend and here we are!!
